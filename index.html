Dictionaries

More than 30 new translations with new chapters being translated every month! We use both translations compiled by our team and edited outdated translations from various years. Any translators who want to join are welcome.

Translations

Connections with numerous universities and institutions. Our team consists of both humanitarian specialists from various educational institutes and normal dudes who work together to present you accurate and accessible content.

New search tools added on our latest dictionary!

12 December 2019

We've been working on updating our obsolete dictionary search system for quite a while now and we're glad to announce that it recieved its official update!

From now on you can search not only by lemma but also in the dictionary article itself. Another new useful feature is the search in all our main languages - English, Latin, Ancient Greek, Sanskrit and Bulgarian using the language's writing system without transliteration.

Black Friday discount now!

20 November 2019

In respect of the coming hilidays we've prepared some discounts for our users which will be available until the last day of November. All our dictionaries' monthly subscription is now 50 cents per user or 5 euro for the whole institution.

New translations of Lucian available

3 April 2018

We've added some of our favorite works by Lucian in our translations base - namely "The Dead Come to Life or The Fisherman", "The Double Indictment or Trials by Jury" and "The Dream or Lucian's Career".

For now they are only available for users who provide valid institutional ID until the copyright questions are solved. Until then check out the rest of our Lucian collection.
