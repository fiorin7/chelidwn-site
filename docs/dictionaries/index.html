The sources

The collection consists mostly of digitized copyright-free works.

The physical copies of many of them were donated by various libraries and institutions.

Some of our copies were so old that they were falling apart. We had to work on restoring the books themselves first.

The team

Students, dogs, professors and software engineers came todether to make these works more accessible.

With small exceptions neither of them accepted any monetary reward for their labor. They were happy to work for just one vafla Borovets a day.

Donations

The maintenance of the corpus is only possible through out reader's donations.

They pay both for the servers and for the human labor needed to keep our site up to date.

We kindly ask you do donate some small sum, if possible, to help us keep up.
